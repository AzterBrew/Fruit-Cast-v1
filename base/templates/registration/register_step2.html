Fruit Cast

Bataan, which boasts a thriving agricultural sector, is home to a wide range of seasonal fruits that contribute to the local economy. The rich environment presents the farmers with the opportunity to plant fruits (i.e., watermelon, pineapple, avocado, pomelo) depending on the season. Watermelon, for example, thrive during the summer, while fruits like rambutan thrive during the rainy season—this cycle ensures a year-round production for the fruit farming sector that not only supports the farmers livelihoods but also boosts their town’s economic growth.
